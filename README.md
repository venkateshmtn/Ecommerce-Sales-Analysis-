Ecommerce Sales Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

Project Overview

This project analyzes e-commerce sales data to uncover insights into revenue trends, product performance, and customer purchasing behavior.
The dashboard provides interactive KPIs to support business decision-making.

Tools & Technologies Used: SQL – Data extraction and transformation, Python – Data cleaning & preprocessing, Power BI – Data modeling, DAX measures & visualization, Excel/CSV – Data source

Key Business Insights: Total Revenue & Total Orders tracking, Monthly & Yearly sales trends, Top-selling products & categories, Customer segmentation analysis, Region-wise sales performance, Profit margin analysis

Dashboard Features: Interactive slicers (Date, Category, Region), KPI Cards (Revenue, Orders, Profit, AOV), Drill-through analysis, Trend & comparison charts, Dynamic DAX calculations

Business Impact: Identified high-revenue product categories, Highlighted underperforming regions, Improved visibility into customer purchasing patterns, Supported strategic pricing & marketing decisions
